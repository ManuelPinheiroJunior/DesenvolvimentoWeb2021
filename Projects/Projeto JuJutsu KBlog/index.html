Satoru Gojo

Satoru Gojo (五 ご 条 じ ょ う 悟 さ と る Gojō Satoru) is one of the protagonists of Jujutsu Kaisen. He is a professor at the Tokyo Prefecture Jujutsu High School.Satoru Gojo is a tall man, much taller than his students,and is apparently considered attractive by many women.He has white hair that is usually spiked up, but Gojo lets him loose when in a more casual outfit.Gojo has bright blue eyes, but they are usually covered by their characteristic black blindfold or a pair of sunglasses.

Megumi Fushiguro

Student the Gojo

Megumi Fushiguro (伏 ふ し 黒 ぐ ろ 恵 め ぐ み Fushiguro Megumi) is one of the protagonists of the Jujutsu Kaisen series.He is a first year student at Jujutsu College and also a descendant of the Zenin Family.Megumi is a tall boy,with dark hair and light green eyes.He wears the standard uniform of the Tokyo Prefecture Jujutsu High School,which consists of a long-sleeved shirt and a tall dark-colored collar, dark pants and shoes.
